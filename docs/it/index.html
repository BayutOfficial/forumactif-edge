Un tema moderno per Forumattivo

Forumactif Edge is a modern mobile-friendly theme built by the community of Forumotion. Created with the mindset of one version for all devices.

Mobile-friendly

Browse and chat on any device, from anywhere, at anytime, without any hassle.

Design moderno

Flat and spacious design that's easy on the eyes and fits any screen.

Installa Forumactif-Edge

For the free forum service, Forumotion.

Funzionalità

Forumactif Edge is full-featured, right out of the box. Providing you with a handful of plugins for everyday foruming.

Collapsible Sticky Widgets

Widgets are stored in a side menu that's always at your finger tips. Giving you easier accessibility and a more spacious forum.

Navigazione veloce

The navbar follows you as you scroll, so you never have to scroll back to the top of the page.

Dynamic Theme Changer

Change the theme on the fly with our theme selector, that allows your members to change the color of the forum to the one they prefer!

Modalità notte

Turn the lights off for some late night browsing, and make things easier on your eyes. Dark Mode gives your members the power to change the lightness of the forum!

Contatore voti

Metti "Mi piace" o "non mi piace" senza dover cambiare pagina. Puoi anche vedere quanti voti ha ricevuto un post!

Post colorati

I post sono colorati in base al gruppo a cui appartieni, per permettere un miglior riconoscimento del gruppo in cui ti trovi.

Visualizza tutte le funzioni

Temi

Forumactif Edge provides you with both a dark and light theme, along with a plethora of colors to choose from, giving you the ability to truly personalize your forum.

Chiaro e Scuro

Choose from a professional light theme, or a sophisticated dark theme. Two designs to match any taste!

100+ Temi di colori

Scegli tra migliaia di diversi colori, o crea il tuo.

Personalizza

Import an editable stylesheet and personalize it however you like. Plus all edited templates are at your finger tips!

Destra-verso-sinistra

Themes support a right-to-left version, which makes Forumactif Edge a perfect choice for rtl scripts.

Pannello di controllo

Forumactif Edge comes with a smart automated control panel that helps you with installation and other everyday tasks, making installation and customization a breeze.

Installa e disinstalla

Our Smart Control Panel helps you Install or Uninstall Forumactif Edge, without you having to ever lift a finger.

Controlla per aggiornamenti

When a new version of Forumactif Edge is available on Github, our Smart Control Panel will notify you and help you install the latest release.

Cambia lingua

You can change the language of Forumactif Edge at any time. Just select the language, click "Change Language", and the Control Panel does the rest!

Temi e gestione impostazioni

Changing the forum width, themes, etc.. is a breeze with our Smart Control Panel. Just select your preferred settings, save, and you're done!
